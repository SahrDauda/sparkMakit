## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

SparkMakit is a comprehensive multi-vendor e-commerce platform designed to connect vendors from Sierra Leone and the African continent with a global customer base. The platform enables small to medium-sized businesses to establish an online presence without the technical complexity or high costs typically associated with e-commerce operations.

**Core Value Proposition:**
- Vendors: Low-cost, easy-to-use platform to reach global markets
- Customers: Access to authentic, quality products from verified sellers
- Platform: Sustainable commission-based revenue model

### 2.1 Target Markets

**Primary Market:**
- Geographic: Sierra Leone, West Africa
- Demographics: Age 18-45, urban and peri-urban residents
- Psychographics: Tech-savvy, entrepreneurial, seeking convenience

**Secondary Market:**
- Sierra Leone diaspora globally
- African diaspora interested in authentic products
- International customers seeking unique African products

### 2.2 Market Opportunity

**Market Size:**
- Sierra Leone e-commerce market: $50M (2024), projected $200M by 2027
- West African e-commerce: $15B (2024), projected $75B by 2030
- Global ethnic marketplace segment: $500B+

**Market Gaps:**
- Limited accessible e-commerce platforms for small vendors
- High barriers to entry (technical, financial)
- Lack of trust in online transactions
- Poor user experience on existing platforms
- Limited payment options for local markets
