## FOR CUSTOMERS: HOW TO BUY PRODUCTS

### STEP 1: GETTING STARTED

**Visit the Website:**
1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Safari, or Firefox)
2. Type in the SparkMakit website address
3. You'll see the homepage with a big picture showing different products

**Understanding the Homepage:**
- **Top Menu Bar:** This is at the very top with the SparkMakit logo
  - Click "Home" to go back to the main page
  - Click "Products" to see all products
  - Click "About Us" to learn about SparkMakit
  - You'll see a shopping cart icon (üõí) - this shows your items
  - You'll see a person icon (üë§) - click this to login

**The Main Picture Area (Hero Section):**
- You'll see big beautiful pictures that change automatically
- There's a "Start Shopping" button - click it to see all products
- There's a "Become a Vendor" button - click this if you want to sell products

### STEP 2: BROWSING PRODUCTS

**Looking at Product Categories:**

As you scroll down the homepage, you'll see different sections:

1. **ELECTRONICS & GADGETS**
   - Headphones, phones, computers, speakers
   - Swipe left/right on your phone to see more items

2. **HOME & KITCHEN**
   - Water bottles, coffee mugs, candles, kitchen tools
   - Swipe to see more products

3. **SPORTS & FITNESS**
   - Yoga mats, running shoes, fitness watches
   - Swipe through the products

4. **FASHION & APPAREL**
   - Shirts, dresses, jackets, pants
   - Keep swiping to see everything

5. **ACCESSORIES**
   - Bags, belts, wallets, sunglasses
   - More items to browse through

6. **JEWELRY**
   - Necklaces, earrings, bracelets, rings
   - Beautiful items to choose from

**How to Swipe:**
- On your phone: Touch the product and drag left or right
- On computer: Use the arrows on the sides or scroll sideways with your mouse

### STEP 3: VIEWING ALL PRODUCTS

**Click "Products" at the top menu or click "Start Shopping"**

You'll now see a page with ALL products displayed in a grid:

**On Your Phone:**
- At the top, you'll see dropdown menus:
  - **Categories** - Click to choose: Electronics, Fashion, Jewelry, etc.
  - **Price Range** - Set how much you want to spend
  - **Country** - Filter by where products come from
- These filters stay at the top even when you scroll down

**On Computer:**
- The filters are on the left side of the screen
- Check the boxes next to what you want
- Use the slider to set your price range

**How to Filter Products:**
1. Click "Categories" dropdown
2. Select the category you want (e.g., "Fashion")
3. Products will automatically update to show only Fashion items
4. You can choose multiple filters at once

### STEP 4: VIEWING A PRODUCT

**Click on any product card to see full details**

**Product Details Page Shows:**

**LEFT SIDE:**
- Big product picture
- Small pictures at the bottom - click any to see it larger
- Click the main picture to zoom in

**RIGHT SIDE:**
- Product name (what it's called)
- Price in NLe (New Leone - Sierra Leone currency)
- Star rating (how other customers rated it)
- Number of reviews
- Vendor name (who's selling it)
- Stock availability (how many are left)

**Product Options:**
- **Size/Color Selection:** Click the button for your size or color
- **Quantity:** Click + or - to choose how many you want
- **Add to Cart Button:** The big blue button - click this to add the item
- **Wishlist Heart:** Click the heart icon to save for later

**More Information Tabs:**
- **Description:** What the product is all about
- **Specifications:** Technical details
- **Reviews:** What other customers are saying
- **Shipping:** How it will be delivered
- **Care Instructions:** How to maintain the product

**Vendor Information Box:**
- Shows the store selling this product
- Their rating and number of reviews
- When they joined SparkMakit
- "View Store" button - click to see all their products

**Related Products:**
- At the bottom, you'll see similar products
- Swipe through these for more options

### STEP 5: ADDING TO CART

**After clicking "Add to Cart":**
1. The cart icon (üõí) at the top will show a number
2. This number tells you how many items are in your cart
3. Click the cart icon anytime to see what you've added

### STEP 6: VIEWING YOUR CART

**Click the cart icon (üõí) at the top**

**On the Cart Page:**

**LEFT SIDE (or top on phone):**
- All products you added
- Each product shows:
  - Picture
  - Name
  - Price per item
  - Quantity (you can change this with + and -)
  - Total price for that item
  - X button to remove item

**RIGHT SIDE (or bottom on phone):**
- **Order Summary:**
  - Subtotal (all items added together)
  - Shipping cost
  - **Total Amount** (what you'll pay)
- **Promo Code Box:** If you have a discount code, enter it here
- **"Proceed to Checkout" Button:** Click this to buy

### STEP 7: CHECKOUT (PAYING FOR YOUR ITEMS)

**After clicking "Proceed to Checkout":**

The checkout page has 3 steps:

**STEP 1: YOUR INFORMATION**
- Enter your name
- Enter your email address (so we can send updates)
- Enter your phone number

**STEP 2: DELIVERY ADDRESS**
- Street address (where you live)
- City
- State/Province
- ZIP/Postal Code
- Country

**STEP 3: SHIPPING METHOD**
Choose how you want your items delivered:
- **Standard Shipping** (5-7 days) - Cheaper
- **Express Shipping** (2-3 days) - Faster but costs more
- **Overnight Delivery** (1 day) - Most expensive but quickest

**STEP 4: PAYMENT METHOD**
Choose how to pay:
- **Credit/Debit Card**
  - Enter card number
  - Expiry date
  - CVV (3 digits on back of card)
  - Cardholder name
  
- **PayPal**
  - Click PayPal button
  - Login to your PayPal account
  - Confirm payment

**Review Your Order:**
- Check everything is correct
- See your total amount
- Click "Place Order" button

### STEP 8: ORDER CONFIRMATION

**Success! You'll see:**
- A green checkmark ‚úì
- "Order Placed Successfully" message
- Your order number (write this down!)
- Confirmation sent to your email

**What Happens Next:**
1. The vendor will prepare your items
2. You'll get an email when it ships
3. You'll receive a tracking number
4. Your items will arrive at your address

## FOR VENDORS: HOW TO SELL PRODUCTS

### BECOMING A VENDOR

**STEP 1: REGISTER AS A VENDOR**

1. **Go to the Homepage**
2. **Click "Become a Vendor"** button (in the big picture area)
3. **Or click "Vendor Login"** at the top right, then click "Apply Now"

**You'll See a Registration Form:**

**SECTION 1: PERSONAL INFORMATION**
- Full Name: Your complete name
- Email Address: Your email (you'll use this to login)
- Phone Number: Your contact number
- Password: Create a strong password
- Confirm Password: Type the same password again

**SECTION 2: BUSINESS DETAILS**
- Business Name: What you want to call your store
- Business Type: Choose from dropdown (Individual, Partnership, Company)
- Business Registration Number: If you have one
- Tax ID Number: If applicable

**SECTION 3: STORE INFORMATION**
- Store Name: The name customers will see
- Store Description: Tell customers about your store (what you sell, what makes you special)
- Store Category: What type of products (Electronics, Fashion, etc.)
- Business Address: Your physical location
- City
- State/Province
- ZIP Code
- Country

**SECTION 4: DOCUMENTS (Upload Pictures)**
- Business License: Take a photo of your business license
- Tax Certificate: Upload your tax documents
- ID/Passport: Upload a picture of your ID

**Click "Submit Application"**

**What Happens Next:**
- Our team will review your application
- This usually takes 2-3 business days
- You'll get an email when approved
- Then you can login to your vendor portal

### STEP 2: LOGGING INTO VENDOR PORTAL

**After Your Application is Approved:**

1. **Click "Vendor Login"** at the top of the website
2. **Enter Your Email**
3. **Enter Your Password**
4. **Click "Login"**

**You'll See Your Vendor Dashboard!**

### STEP 3: UNDERSTANDING YOUR VENDOR DASHBOARD

**On Your Phone:**
- Click the three lines (‚ò∞) at the top left to open the menu

**On Computer:**
- The menu is already open on the left side

**MENU OPTIONS:**

**üìä Dashboard** (Main Page)
- See your important numbers:
  - Total Products: How many products you're selling
  - Total Sales: How much money you've made
  - Pending Orders: Orders waiting to be processed
  - Total Revenue: All money earned

- **Sales Graph:** Shows your sales over time
  - You can see daily, weekly, or monthly sales
  - The graph goes up when you make more sales!

- **Recent Orders:** Shows the latest orders from customers
- **Top Products:** Your best-selling items

**üì¶ Products** (Manage Your Products)
- See all products you're selling
- Add new products
- Edit existing products
- Remove products

**üìä Analytics** (See Your Performance)
- Revenue trends: How much you're making over time
- Popular products: What customers buy most
- Customer insights: Where your customers are from
- Performance metrics: How well you're doing

### STEP 4: ADDING A NEW PRODUCT

**From the Dashboard or Products Page:**

1. **Click "Add Product"** button (usually at the top right)
2. **A Form Will Appear (Modal/Pop-up)**

**FILL IN THE PRODUCT INFORMATION:**

**Basic Information:**
- **Product Name:** What you're selling (e.g., "Wireless Headphones")
- **Category:** Choose from dropdown (Electronics, Fashion, etc.)
- **Description:** Explain what the product is
  - What it does
  - What makes it special
  - Why customers should buy it

**Pricing:**
- **Price:** How much it costs in NLe (New Leone)
- **Compare At Price:** If it was more expensive before (for showing discounts)

**Inventory:**
- **Stock Quantity:** How many you have to sell
- **SKU:** Product code (optional - you can create your own system)

**Images:**
- **Upload Pictures:** Click to add photos of your product
  - Take clear, bright photos
  - Show the product from different angles
  - First photo will be the main picture customers see

**Shipping:**
- **Weight:** How heavy the product is
- **Dimensions:** Size (length, width, height)

**Product Variants (if applicable):**
- If your product comes in different sizes or colors
- Click "Add Variant"
- Enter size (S, M, L) or color options

**Click "Save" or "Add Product"**

**Your product is now live!** Customers can see it and buy it!

### STEP 5: MANAGING YOUR PRODUCTS

**Go to "Products" in Your Menu:**

You'll see a table with all your products:

**Each Product Shows:**
- Picture
- Product Name
- Price
- Stock (how many left)
- Status (Active or Inactive)

**What You Can Do:**

**Edit a Product:**
1. Click the pencil/edit icon
2. Change any information
3. Click "Save Changes"

**Delete a Product:**
1. Click the trash/delete icon
2. Confirm you want to delete
3. Product is removed

**View Product:**
- Click the eye icon to see how customers see it

**Make Product Active/Inactive:**
- Toggle the switch to turn it on/off
- Off means customers can't see it
- Useful when you're out of stock

### STEP 6: VIEWING ANALYTICS

**Click "Analytics" in Your Menu:**

**You'll See:**

**Revenue Tracking:**
- Graph showing money earned over time
- Can view by day, week, month, or year
- Total revenue number at the top

**Performance Metrics:**
- Top selling products
- Total orders
- Average order value
- Conversion rate (how many visitors buy)

**Customer Insights:**
- Where customers are from
- What times they shop most
- What categories they prefer

**Product Performance:**
- Which products sell best
- Which need promotion
- Which are slow sellers

**How This Helps You:**
- Know what's selling well (stock up on these!)
- Know what's not selling (maybe lower the price or improve the listing)
- Understand your customers better
- Plan your inventory

### STEP 7: BEST PRACTICES FOR VENDORS

**TO BE SUCCESSFUL ON SPARKMAKIT:**

**1. Take Great Product Photos:**
- Use good lighting (natural daylight is best)
- Clean background (white or neutral colors)
- Show product from multiple angles
- Include size reference if needed

**2. Write Clear Descriptions:**
- Explain exactly what the product is
- List all features and benefits
- Be honest about any limitations
- Use simple language

**3. Price Fairly:**
- Research similar products
- Don't price too high (customers will skip)
- Don't price too low (you need to make profit)
- Consider your costs and desired profit margin

**4. Keep Stock Updated:**
- Update quantities when items sell out
- Mark out-of-stock items as inactive
- Restock popular items quickly

**5. Respond to Orders Quickly:**
- Check for new orders daily
- Process orders within 24 hours
- Communicate with customers if there are delays

**6. Maintain Good Ratings:**
- Provide accurate descriptions
- Ship items on time
- Package items safely
- Provide good customer service

## GLOSSARY (IMPORTANT WORDS)

- **Cart:** Your shopping basket where items you want to buy are stored
- **Checkout:** The process of paying for your items
- **Dashboard:** Your main control page (for vendors)
- **Filter:** Tool to narrow down products by category, price, etc.
- **Inventory:** The stock/quantity of products available
- **Modal:** A pop-up window on the screen
- **NLe:** New Leone - Sierra Leone's currency
- **Portal:** Special area for vendors/admins to manage their activities
- **SKU:** Stock Keeping Unit - unique code for a product
- **Vendor:** A seller/business owner who sells products on SparkMakit
- **Wishlist:** Saved products you might want to buy later
